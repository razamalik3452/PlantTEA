Some common questions
were often asked

**How often should I water my plants?**

Most plants thrive with a consistent watering schedule, typically once a week, but it varies based on species and environmental factors.

**What is the best light for indoor plants?**

Most indoor plants prefer bright, indirect light, while some low-light species can tolerate shadier conditions.

**How do I prevent pests on my plants?**

Regularly inspect your plants for signs of pests like yellowing leaves or tiny insects. Use natural remedies or insecticidal soap to control infestations.

**Can I repot my plant into a larger pot?**

Repotting is beneficial when a plant becomes root-bound, typically every 1-2 years. Choose a slightly larger pot with proper drainage.

**Why are my plant's leaves turning brown?**

Browning leaves can indicate overwatering, underwatering, or low humidity. Adjust your care routine accordingly to restore leaf health.

**What is the best fertilizer for my plants?**

Balanced, water-soluble fertilizers with a ratio like 10-10-10 or 20-20-20 work well for most plants. Follow the package instructions for proper application.
